Welcome to Total Tool Supply, Inc.

Total Tool Supply, is a contractor and industrial supply distribution company that provdies tools, services and solutions to a large and diverse customer base across North America.

In business since 1977, Total Tool is dedicated to working in partnership with professional grade manufacturers to bring the best product and services for the companies that build and maintain facilities and systems.

Total Tool Supply has locations in St. Paul and Hibbing Minneasota; Superior, Wausau and Appleton, Wisconsin; Lincoln and Omanha, Nebraska; Des Moines, Lowa; Kansas City, Kansas; and Ishpeming, Michigan. With a focus on new tool sales, rental, in-house repair, rigging and lifting products and a crane & hoist division, total tool is well known as a one-stop destination for its valued customer base. Be sure to check out our specials & promotions!

Why Work with Total Tool?

Total Tool has the tools to get the job done right the first time, safely, on time and within budget. The people of Total Tool have the product and application knowledge to find the perfect solution, source the tools and supplies, and deliver the complete package to the job site or company location.

Our Goal at Total Tool

Our goal is to be the preferred supplier to the construction and industrial customers that we serve. To that end, we work to develop partnerships with our customers and suppliers that are mutually beneficial.
