Rental

Think of Total Tool when you need to rent

For out of town jobs, big jobs or when your tools are in for repair there are times when renting your tools and equipment just makes the most sense. Turn to Total Tool for high quality, up to date equipment delivered right to your jobsite!

Rental Packages

We can rent you a package of tools and equipment for the whole job, from hand tools to generators to compressors. Perfect for short term jobs, plant shutdowns, and turnarounds. Call for pricing on a standard or custom package. Complete repair service avaiable. Drop off a tool for repair and rent a replacement tool at the same time.

Rental Rates

Based on the following: one day is 24 hours, one week is 5 consecutive days and one month is 28 consecutive days. To help keep our rates reasonable, the minimum rental charge is one day.

Care of Equipment

Total Tool rents equipment with the understanding that some normal wear and tear will take place and is included in the basic rental rates. Renter is responsible for theft, vandalism, flat tires, negligence, abuse, misuse, routine maintenance, fuel and cleaning charges necessary to return the equipment in the same condition as the beginning of the rental.

Deposits

Unless pre-approved credit has been established with Total Tool, rental deposits and proper I.D. are required for each item. We will be happy to provide you with a Total Tool credit application at any time.
