Staff Accountant

Staff Accountant

Total Tool Supply is seeking an experienced Staff Accountant to join our finance team.

Overview

The Staff Accountant will be responsible for preparation of financial analysis and reporting, developing annual budgets, working closely with auditors and will be the primary contact for accounting issues and transactions.

Essential functions and responsibilities of the Staff Accountant include:

  • Prepare accounting journals
  • Consolidation of accounting records for 13 branch locations.
  • Support accounts payable and accounts receivable activities to ensure accuracy and timeliness in recording transactions.
  • Account reconciliations.
  • Provide detailed analyses of transactions.
  • Process and/or monitor various banking and cash transaction activities.
  • Preparation of financial reports.
  • Manage sales tax process and reconcile/resolve issues as needed.
  • Analyze results to better understand the financial performance of the company.
  • Effectively communicate financial information across all levels of the organization.
  • Assist with administration of payroll and payroll tax processes.
  • Support vehicle fleet administration.
  • Maintain fixed asset records.
  • Special projects for CFO as assigned.

Competencies and skill requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting
  • 4+ years’ experience in accounting, preferably in a wholesale environment
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Strong analytical skills.
  • Able to read and understand technical financial reports.
  • Understanding of accounting and internal control processes.
  • Willingness to dig into details, perform multiple tasks
  • Experience working with and developing ERP reporting and systems.
