About Us

Total Tool Supply, Inc. is a contractor and industrial supply distribution company that provides tools, services and solutions to a large and diverse customer base across North America.

Total Tool Supply, Inc is a family owned company providing tools, services and solutions to construction and industrial customers across North America. We have a long history of delivering the right package to get the job done safely, on time and on budget. Our exclusive partnerships with professional grade manufacturers allow us to bring the best products and services to those who build and maintain facilities and systems. We do all of this while maintaining competitive pricing and excellent customer service. This is who we are and how we do business.

We are your ONE SOURCE for construction and industrial needs

At Total Tool Supply, Inc our goal is to be the preferred supplier to the construction and industrial customers we serve. To that end, we work to create partnerships with our customers and suppliers that are mutually beneficial. We aim to develop the best people, provide the best products and offer the best service. We deal with our customers, associates and suppliers with integrity and respect.

History

1977

January 1, 1977 Total Tool Supply, Inc. is established by Doug Jordan and his partners in Saint Paul, MN with one location and 3 employees.

1980's

Total Tool begins to offer Rental and Repair services and acquires current HQ building in St. Paul, MN

1990's

Total Tool purchases Safeway Sling and begins to offer Rigging services and products. The company continues to grow and in the mid-90’s opens branches in Superior, Wausau and Appleton, Wisconsin. Total Tool launches the Crane & Hoist division.

2000's

Between 2005-11, Total Tool adds six more branches to include Hibbing in 2005, the purchase of The Tool House in 2007 and the opening of the Des Moines, IA, Lincoln, NE and Omaha, NE locations. The Ishpeming, MI branch opened in 2008 and Kansas City, KS followed 2011.

2007

In 2007 Doug Jordan transitions to company CEO and steps away from day-to-day operations and Andy Jordan becomes President of Total Tool Supply.

2017

Total Tool acquires Bailey Tools & Safety and adds two new locations: Louisville, Kentucky (Bailey Tools & Safety A Total Tool Supply Company) and Indianapolis, Indiana. Total Tool currently has 12 locations and approximately 200 employees
